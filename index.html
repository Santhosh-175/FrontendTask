Our Story

Two decades of sustainable excellence

From Nature to Nurture

Since 2003, Aruna Coir Industries has been pioneering sustainable coir solutions, turning agricultural waste into valuable products that benefit farmers, gardeners, and the environment.

Located in Eluru, Andhra Pradesh, our eco-friendly facility processes over 300 tons of coconut husk monthly, creating premium coir products that serve markets across India and internationally.

We believe in the power of nature and the importance of sustainable practices. Every product we create is a testament to our commitment to environmental stewardship and quality craftsmanship.
